About MCA

Why My Concert App?

I didn't realize how much I loved live music until I married a girl who loved live music. Having gone to a handful of shows in the years before I met her, my concert attendance has skyrocketed in the years since.

We each kept a textfile or spreadsheet of the concerts we'd been to, but those boring documents couldn't convey the excitement we felt when attending the shows. We both wanted a better way to keep track of all of the concerts we've been to, add a few notes, and maybe upload a few photos to help remember and relive the experience.

MCA (My Concert App) has been in the back of my brain for years. It wasn't only until recently that I've been able to make it tangible. Having enrolled in a Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Nashville Software School (NSS), I had the opportunity to create web applications as part of my education and training.

Hopefully, you get as much enjoyment in using this site as we have.

Rock On!

- MCA
